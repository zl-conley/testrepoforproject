Adoption Process

A valid ID must be presented in order for the adoption process to be start.

The adoption process here at Columbus pet help is fairly easy and takes around 40 minutes to complete once started. Wait times vary depending on the amount of people attempting to adopt. We do suggest getting to know the new pal you are adopting to know if it is a good match for both adopter and animal.

Adoption Process

Once you find an animal that meets your criteria, you are able to start the adoption process. Our team of employees can help determine if you two are a good match then you can move onto the next step.
Through paperwork (in person) or the online adoption forms you may complete the application for your hopefully soon to be furry pal. For online applications go to your pals picture and then click the adopt me button. Once that is done, somebody from our team will ask you questions regarding the adoption to gain more information.
Once a match is made, the adoption will be completed by getting your signature on the paperwork provided when picking up your pal. Please be sure to have proper equipment when picking up your pal (carrier for cats, leash and collar for dogs). To end the process a team member will ensure that all medical information is gone over before taking your furry pal home. Last but not least, enjoy your new furry best friend!
